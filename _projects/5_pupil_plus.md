### Projects

**Pupil+** — A handheld device to quantify and measure pupillary and neurological responses to light stimuli. I developed the full hardware system including:

- Processing unit
- Illumination system (red LED stimulus)
- Battery systems
- I/O units and touch interface

**Infant Gaze Tracking** — Developed a system to quantify infant response to changing light stimuli without subjectivity, enabling more reliable pediatric eye exams.

**Dry Eye Syndrome Detection** — Developed an inexpensive, multifunctional device to detect and quantify Dry Eye Syndrome.

### Mentorship

I also served as a technical mentor at the 5th and 6th editions of the **"Engineering the Eye" Hackathon** — teams I mentored placed 1st runner-up in both editions.
