### Specifications

- **Height:** 1.72 m (human-scale)
- **Budget:** INR 7 million (USD ~100K)
- **DoF:** Multiple, custom-designed joints
- **Built from scratch** — mechanical design, electronics, and software

### Capabilities Developed

- Walking and active balance control
- Push recovery
- Vertical jumping (presented at ROBIO 2018)
- Real-time teleoperation with full-body motion imitation from human operators (presented at ICARM 2018)
